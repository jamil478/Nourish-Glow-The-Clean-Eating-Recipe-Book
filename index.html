Rise & Shine Breakfasts 🌅

Start your morning with intention. These breakfasts are designed to energize your body, balance your blood sugar, and make you feel absolutely radiant.

Pink Berry Smoothie Bowl

🫐
🕐 10 mins🍽️ Serves 1⚡ ~280 cal
Ingredients
  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 1 frozen banana
  • ½ cup coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • Toppings: granola, fresh berries, honey drizzle
Instructions
  • Blend berries, banana, coconut milk, and chia seeds until thick and perfectly creamy.
  • Pour into your favorite bowl and arrange toppings beautifully. Serve immediately.

Avocado & Egg Toast

🥑
🕐 15 mins🍽️ Serves 1⚡ ~320 cal
Ingredients
  • 2 slices artisan sourdough
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 organic free-range eggs
  • Fresh lemon juice
  • Red pepper flakes, sea salt
  • Microgreens for garnish
Instructions
  • Toast the sourdough slices until perfectly crisp. Mash avocado with lemon and salt.
  • Poach or fry your eggs. Spread avocado on toast, top with eggs, pepper flakes, and greens.

Overnight Oats with Mango

🥭
🕐 5 mins (prep)🍽️ Serves 1⚡ ~350 cal
Ingredients
  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tbsp raw honey
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ ripe mango, diced
  • Toasted coconut flakes
Instructions
  • Combine oats, almond milk, honey, and vanilla in a glass jar. Stir well and seal.
  • Refrigerate overnight. In the morning, layer with fresh diced mango and coconut flakes.

Fresh & Vibrant Salads 🥗

Salads don't have to be boring. These vibrant bowls are packed with color, texture, and flavor — proof that eating clean is anything but dull.

Strawberry Spinach Salad

🍓
🕐 10 mins🍽️ Serves 2⚡ ~220 cal
Ingredients
  • 3 cups fresh baby spinach
  • 1 cup sliced strawberries
  • ¼ cup crumbled feta cheese
  • ¼ cup raw walnuts
  • 2 tbsp balsamic glaze
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
Instructions
  • Toss the baby spinach, strawberries, feta, and walnuts together gently in a wide bowl.
  • Drizzle olive oil and rich balsamic glaze evenly. Season with cracked pepper; serve immediately.

Rainbow Buddha Bowl

🌈
🕐 20 mins🍽️ Serves 2⚡ ~420 cal
Ingredients
  • 1 cup cooked fluffy quinoa
  • 1 cup chickpeas, oven-roasted
  • Shredded purple cabbage
  • Shredded carrot, cucumber
  • Edamame, sesame seeds
  • Creamy tahini dressing
Instructions
  • Roast chickpeas at 200°C for 20 mins until crisp. Prepare and clean all fresh vegetables.
  • Arrange elements in sections over a base of quinoa. Drizzle with creamy tahini & sesame seeds.

Greek Cucumber Salad

🥒
🕐 10 mins🍽️ Serves 2⚡ ~180 cal
Ingredients
  • 2 English cucumbers, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ½ crisp red onion, sliced
  • ½ cup kalamata olives
  • ½ cup premium feta cheese
  • Dill, lemon juice, olive oil
Instructions
  • Combine chopped cucumbers, tomatoes, onion, and olives in a bowl. Fold in feta gently.
  • Dress with fresh squeezed lemon juice and rich olive oil. Chill for 15 mins before serving.

Nourishing Soups & Bowls 🍲

Warm, healing, and soul-satisfying. These soups and bowls are liquid comfort that nourishes you from the inside out.

Golden Turmeric Soup

✨
🕐 30 mins🍽️ Serves 4⚡ ~180 cal
Ingredients
  • 1 large head of cauliflower
  • 1 white onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp ginger
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • ½ cup light coconut milk
Instructions
  • Sauté onion and garlic. Add cauliflower chopped pieces, turmeric, aromatic ginger, and broth.
  • Simmer 20 mins. Blend until smooth. Stir in coconut milk and serve with a beautiful swirl on top.

Lemon Lentil Soup

🍋
🕐 35 mins🍽️ Serves 4⚡ ~280 cal
Ingredients
  • 1 cup dry red lentils
  • 1 onion, 2 carrots, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp coriander
  • Juice of 2 fresh lemons
  • 5 cups hot vegetable broth
Instructions
  • Sauté standard veggies and garlic. Add well-rinsed lentils, dry spices, and vegetable broth.
  • Simmer for 25 mins. Add fresh lemon juice. Blend partially for an ultra-creamy, comforting texture.

Miso Ginger Noodle Bowl

🍜
🕐 20 mins🍽️ Serves 2⚡ ~320 cal
Ingredients
  • 2 tbsp white miso paste
  • 1 tbsp freshly grated ginger
  • 4 cups organic vegetable broth
  • Rice noodles, fresh bok choy
  • 1 soft-boiled egg, sliced nori
  • Toasted sesame oil, scallions
Instructions
  • Whisk miso paste gently into warm broth. Add ginger. Cook rice noodles separately according to packet.
  • Blanch bok choy. Assemble bowl with noodles, broth, greens, egg, and nori. Finish with sesame oil.

Clean Wraps & Light Lunches 🥙

Midday meals that keep you light, energized, and satisfied — no afternoon slump allowed.

Turkey & Avocado Lettuce Wraps

🥬
🕐 15 mins🍽️ Serves 2⚡ ~290 cal
Ingredients
  • 8 large crisp butter lettuce leaves
  • 200g premium sliced turkey breast
  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced thin
  • 1 cup shredded carrots & cucumber
  • Organic hummus
  • Fresh lemon juice
Instructions
  • Wash and dry lettuce leaves thoroughly. Spread a tablespoon of clean hummus on each leaf.
  • Layer turkey, avocado, carrots, and cucumber sticks. Squeeze lemon on top, wrap gently and serve.

Quinoa Stuffed Bell Peppers

🫑
🕐 40 mins🍽️ Serves 2⚡ ~380 cal
Ingredients
  • 2 large bell peppers, cut in half
  • 1 cup cooked seasoned quinoa
  • 1 can black beans, well rinsed
  • 1 cup sweet corn
  • 1 tsp cumin, fresh chunky salsa
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
Instructions
  • Preheat oven to 190°C. Mix quinoa, beans, corn, cumin, and a few spoons of salsa together.
  • Fill pepper halves tightly. Bake for 25 minutes. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving hot.

Tuna & Chickpea Salad Wrap

🌯
🕐 10 mins🍽️ Serves 2⚡ ~340 cal
Ingredients
  • 1 can tuna in water, drained
  • 1 can chickpeas, rinsed & mashed
  • Diced celery & red onion
  • 3 tbsp unsweetened Greek yogurt
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard, lemon juice
  • Whole wheat wraps, baby spinach
Instructions
  • Combine tuna, mashed chickpeas, celery, onion, yogurt, mustard, and lemon juice. Season well.
  • Spread mixture evenly on a whole wheat wrap over a bed of spinach. Roll tight and slice.

Protein-Packed Mains 🐟

These satisfying mains give your body the protein it craves while keeping everything clean, colorful, and absolutely delicious.

Lemon Herb Baked Salmon

🐟
🕐 25 mins🍽️ Serves 2⚡ ~420 cal
Ingredients
  • 2 wild-caught salmon fillets
  • 2 lemons, sliced thin
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • Fresh dill & flat parsley
  • 2 tbsp premium olive oil
  • Sea salt, pepper, capers
Instructions
  • Preheat oven to 200°C. Lay salmon on a baking sheet layered with half the fresh lemon slices.
  • Mix oil, garlic, and herbs. Spread over salmon. Bake for 15-18 mins. Serve with capers.

Chicken & Veggie Stir-Fry

🥢
🕐 25 mins🍽️ Serves 3⚡ ~380 cal
Ingredients
  • 500g lean chicken breast, sliced
  • Broccoli, snap peas, bell pepper
  • 3 tbsp tamari (gluten-free soy)
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil, 1 tbsp honey
  • Grated ginger & garlic
  • Cooked brown rice, sesame seeds
Instructions
  • Whisk tamari, honey, ginger, and garlic. Stir-fry chicken in sesame oil until fully golden.
  • Add crisp veggies; cook 5 mins. Pour sauce over, toss to coat. Serve over healthy brown rice.

Black Bean & Sweet Potato Tacos

🌮
🕐 30 mins🍽️ Serves 3⚡ ~360 cal
Ingredients
  • 2 sweet potatoes, cubed small
  • 1 can clean black beans
  • Organic corn tortillas
  • Shredded red cabbage, avocado
  • Greek yogurt, chipotle, cumin
  • Lime juice, fresh cilantro
Instructions
  • Roast sweet potatoes with cumin and chipotle at 200°C for 20 mins. Warm up your corn tortillas.
  • Layer beans, sweet potato cubes, cabbage, and avocado. Top with yogurt, cilantro, and lime.

Feel-Good Sides & Snacks 🥦

Smart snacking is the secret to staying on track. These sides and snacks are as satisfying as they are nourishing.

Roasted Chickpeas (3 Ways)

🫘
🕐 30 mins🍽️ Serves 4⚡ ~180 cal
Ingredients
  • 2 cans chickpeas, drained
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • Smoky BBQ: paprika, cumin, garlic
  • Lemon Herb: lemon zest, rosemary
  • Spicy: sriracha, lime juice
Instructions
  • Dry chickpeas perfectly with a towel. Toss with olive oil and your choice of spice blend.
  • Bake at 200°C for 25-30 mins, shaking the pan halfway through, until completely crunchy.

Cucumber Hummus Bites

🥒
🕐 10 mins🍽️ Serves 4⚡ ~120 cal
Ingredients
  • 2 large English cucumbers
  • 1 cup premium smooth hummus
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Kalamata olives, chopped
  • Za'atar spice, paprika
  • Fresh flat parsley
Instructions
  • Slice cucumbers into thick 1cm rounds. Pipe or spoon hummus neatly onto each round.
  • Top with a tomato half or olive piece. Dust with za'atar and garnish with fresh parsley.

Sweet Potato Toast

🍠
🕐 20 mins🍽️ Serves 2⚡ ~200 cal
Ingredients
  • 1 large sweet potato
  • Combo 1: Almond butter + banana
  • Combo 2: Avocado + everything spice
  • Combo 3: Clean ricotta + berries
Instructions
  • Slice sweet potato lengthwise into 0.5cm thick slabs. Toast in toaster 2-3 times until tender.
  • Alternatively, bake at 220°C for 15 mins. Load with your favorite combo toppings.

Glow-Boosting Smoothies 🥤

Drink your way to glowing skin, sustained energy, and deep nourishment. These smoothies are liquid gold.

The Glow Smoothie 🌟

✨
🕐 5 mins🍽️ Serves 1⚡ ~240 cal
Ingredients
  • 1 cup frozen mango chunks
  • ½ cup sweet pineapple
  • 1 medium carrot, sliced
  • 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp ginger
  • 1 tbsp premium collagen powder
  • 1 cup pure coconut water
Instructions
  • Add all raw ingredients into a high-speed blender. Blend for 60 seconds until silky.
  • Pour into a beautiful tall glass, dust with a pinch of extra turmeric, and enjoy instantly.

Berry Bliss Smoothie 💜

🫐
🕐 5 mins🍽️ Serves 1⚡ ~280 cal
Ingredients
  • 1 cup organic mixed berries
  • 1 frozen ripe banana
  • 1 tbsp raw almond butter
  • 1 cup creamy oat milk
  • 1 tbsp brown flax seeds
  • Handful of spinach (hidden!)
Instructions
  • Blend all components until fully smooth. Add a tiny splash more oat milk if too thick.
  • Pour and top with a few scattered fresh blueberries. Sip slowly and let your body glow.

Green Goddess Smoothie 🌿

💚
🕐 5 mins🍽️ Serves 1⚡ ~220 cal
Ingredients
  • 2 cups packed baby spinach
  • 1 frozen banana
  • ½ creamy ripe avocado
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tsp pure spirulina powder
  • Juice of 1 lime, ice cubes
Instructions
  • Blend spinach and almond milk first to avoid chunks. Add remaining ingredients.
  • Process until ultra-creamy and vibrant green. Garnish with a fresh lime wheel and mint.

Guilt-Free Desserts 🍫

Life is sweeter with a little indulgence — the clean way. These desserts satisfy your sweet tooth without the guilt or the crash.

Raw Chocolate Bliss Balls

🌰
🕐 15 mins + chill🍽️ Makes 16⚡ ~90 cal each
Ingredients
  • 1 cup pitted Medjool dates
  • 1 cup raw almonds
  • 3 tbsp raw cacao powder
  • 1 tbsp unrefined coconut oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract, sea salt
  • Desiccated coconut to roll
Instructions
  • Process almonds until fine. Add dates, cacao, oil, vanilla, and salt. Pulse until sticky.
  • Roll into neat bite-sized balls, roll in coconut, and refrigerate for 30 minutes to set.

Frozen Mango Yogurt Bark

🥭
🕐 10 mins + freeze🍽️ Serves 6⚡ ~120 cal
Ingredients
  • 2 cups plain Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp raw honey
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 ripe mango, finely diced
  • ½ cup organic berries
  • 2 tbsp grain-free granola
Instructions
  • Mix yogurt, honey, and vanilla. Spread 1cm thick onto a parchment-lined baking tray.
  • Scatter mango, berries, and granola over. Freeze for 3 hours, then break into shards.

Banana Nice Cream

🍦
🕐 5 mins🍽️ Serves 2⚡ ~150 cal
Ingredients
  • 3 large ripe bananas, frozen
  • Strawberry: + 1 cup berries
  • Peanut Butter: + 2 tbsp PB
  • Choc Mint: + cacao nibs & mint
Instructions
  • Chop frozen bananas into chunks. Process in a food processor, scraping down sides often.
  • After 3-4 mins it will turn into a rich soft-serve consistency. Blend in flavor mix-ins.

Your Weekly Meal Prep Guide 📋

Meal prepping is the secret weapon of every clean eater. Spend 2 hours on Sunday, eat like a queen all week.

☀️ Sunday Prep Checklist

  • ⬜ Cook a big batch of quinoa or brown rice
  • ⬜ Roast a giant tray of mixed vegetables
  • ⬜ Hard boil 6 organic eggs for snacks
  • ⬜ Prepare overnight oats for 3 days ahead
  • ⬜ Wash, dry, and chop all salad greens
  • ⬜ Make one big batch of comforting soup
  • ⬜ Portion out smoothie ingredients into bags
  • ⬜ Prep snack boxes (hummus + raw veggies)
💡 Tip 1: Batch cook your proteins like chicken or salmon on Sunday evening to keep assembly under 5 minutes.
💡 Tip 2: Keep your fruits frozen! Frozen fruit makes your smoothies incredibly thick, frosty, and cream-textured.
💡 Tip 3: Prep salad dressings in small mason jars separately. Do not dress salads ahead to avoid soggy leaves!

10 Golden Rules to Live By

  1. 1. 🌱 Eat real food: If you can't pronounce an ingredient listed on the package, skip it.
  2. 2. 💧 Hydrate first: Drink 2 to 3 liters of pure filtered water every single day.
  3. 3. 🌈 Eat the rainbow: A wide variety of colors always equals a wide spectrum of micronutrients.
  4. 4. 🥗 Fill half your plate: Dedicate half of every main plate to fresh greens and non-starchy vegetables.
  5. 5. 🏷️ Read labels: Look at ingredient lists, not marketing claims on the front.
  6. 6. 🍽️ Mindful chewing: Eat slowly, with no screens, and listen to your satiety cues.
  7. 7. 🌙 Close the kitchen: Avoid eating heavy meals 2-3 hours before your bedtime.
  8. 8. 🛒 Shop outer edges: The perimeter of the supermarket holds the freshest, un-boxed whole foods.
  9. 9. 🫙 Prep ahead: Keep healthy alternatives ready so hunger never makes choices for you.
  10. 10. 💕 Progress over perfection: Every individual healthy choice counts towards your permanent glow.
